Hackathon for Uniform Mafia/TechBible/Cherry Ventures/Bright Data.
It was a 90 minute rapid hack, in which I solo built an event management system,
using Bright Data to research popular tech events (from Luma)
and plan an event relevant to your community.
It decomposes the pre-event work into subtasks
and autonomously searches for relevant speakers and drafts request emails to them.
