Developed a system which checks the exposure of a product to the EU AI act,
and outlines actions to take to your tech lead or legal counsel.

This was developed during a hackathon at the London Institute for AI Safety.
The motivation for this was that nobody is ready for the new regulation coming in,
and that will have serious impacts not just on a company's financial situation,
but also their users.
